Requirements

Welcome to the ITS Surabaya Table Tennis Competition!
Date: October, 26th 2023
Location: ITS Surabaya

Agenda Fee
Early Bird Registration
Participants have the opportunity to register early and take advantage of discounted registration fees. Online registration forms are made available on the event's official website.		 Rp. 20.000
Regular Registration
Regular registration period begins, allowing participants to sign up for the competition at the standard registration fee. Online registration forms remain open.		 Rp. 30.000
On-Site Registration
For participants who were unable to register online during the early bird or regular registration periods, on-site registration is available on the day of the event. Participants should arrive early to complete the registration process before the competition starts.		 Rp. 40.000


Participant Requirements

  1. Participants are active students/staff/lecturers of ITS Surabaya.
  2. Participants must provide valid proof of their student or staff status at ITS Surabaya during the registration process.
  3. Participants are only allowed to take part in 1 table tennis competition category (single or double).
  4. Participants must register themselves online on the competition website.
  5. For double category matches, 1 team can come from a different study program and/ faculty.


Rules Of Competition

  1. Participants must be present 1 (one) hour before the competition starts to re-register and take participant numbers.
  2. Participants are required to arrive 30 minutes before the match. If the match has started and the participants are not present on the field, the committee will allow a 5-minute time limit from the specified event, if they are not present, they will be declared WO and the opponent will be awarded the victory.
  3. If a situation like point 2 above occurs involving the two participants facing each other, then both are declared to have lost WO and the opponent in the next round has the right to advance to the next round again.
  4. If the previous match must be played earlier due to a WO, then the next game will immediately continue according to the match schedule. And the participant concerned will be notified by the match leader.
  5. Each participant will be drawn to determine the opponent for the match and the results of the draw cannot be contested.
  6. Participants are expected to remain in place during the competition.
