Timeline

Welcome to the ITS Surabaya Table Tennis Competition!
Date: October, 26th 2023
Location: ITS Surabaya

Date Agenda
September 1st - September 15th Early Bird Registration
Participants have the opportunity to register early and take advantage of discounted registration fees. Online registration forms are made available on the event's official website.
September 16th - October 25th Regular Registration
Regular registration period begins, allowing participants to sign up for the competition at the standard registration fee. Online registration forms remain open.
October 26th On-Site Registration
For participants who were unable to register online during the early bird or regular registration periods, on-site registration is available on the day of the event. Participants should arrive early to complete the registration process before the competition starts.
October 26th Competition Starts
All participants attended the competition.


Here is a tentative timeline for the ITS Surabaya Table Tennis Competition:
8:00 AM - 9:00 AM
Registration and Check-In
Participants arrive at the competition venue and complete the registration process. They receive their player identification tags and tournament information.

9:00 AM - 9:30 AM
Opening Ceremony
The event kicks off with an exciting opening ceremony, featuring speeches from organizers and special guests. The tournament format and rules are explained, and participants are introduced to the enthusiastic crowd.

9:30 AM - 12:00 PM
Singles Preliminary Rounds
The singles category begins with the preliminary rounds. Players are divided into groups and compete against each other in round-robin format matches. The top players from each group advance to the next stage.

12:00 PM - 1:00 PM
Lunch Break
Participants and spectators have a break to enjoy a delicious meal and recharge for the afternoon matches. Food stalls and refreshments are available within the venue.

1:00 PM - 3:30 PM
Doubles Preliminary Rounds
The doubles category commences with the preliminary rounds. Teams are organized into groups and engage in exciting matches to secure their positions for the next phase.

3:30 PM - 4:00 PM
Exhibition Matches and Entertainment
During this interval, spectators are treated to entertaining exhibition matches featuring skilled players. There may also be performances and activities to engage the audience.

4:00 PM - 5:30 PM
Singles and Doubles Elimination Rounds
The competition intensifies as the singles and doubles elimination rounds begin. Players compete in knockout matches, with the winners advancing to the next round while others bid farewell to the competition.

5:30 PM - 6:00 PM
Semi-Finals and Finals
The semi-final matches take place, showcasing the top players in each category. The winners proceed to the finals, where they battle for the ultimate glory and the chance to be crowned champions of ITS Surabaya Table Tennis Competition.

6:00 PM - 6:30 PM
Award Ceremony
The event concludes with a grand award ceremony. Trophies, medals, and prizes are presented to the winners and runners-up in each category. Special acknowledgments are made for outstanding performances and sportsmanship.

Please note that the timeline is subject to change based on the number of participants and the progress of the matches. The exact schedule and duration of each phase will be communicated to the participants prior to the competition.
