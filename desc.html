Description

Welcome to the ITS Surabaya Table Tennis Competition!

Prepare yourself for an exhilarating display of table tennis skills as we bring you the highly anticipated ITS Surabaya Table Tennis Competition. Held at our prestigious institution, this event showcases the incredible talent and passion for table tennis among our participants.


The ITS Surabaya Table Tennis Competition is a thrilling gathering of players from diverse backgrounds, including students, faculty members, and local table tennis enthusiasts. It serves as a platform for players to showcase their abilities and compete in a spirited and friendly environment. As you enter the competition venue, the atmosphere is charged with excitement and anticipation. The sound of bouncing balls and the cheers from spectators create a vibrant energy that sets the stage for intense matches and memorable moments. Witness the finesse and agility of the players as they engage in fast-paced rallies, showcasing their lightning-fast reflexes and precise shot placements. From powerful smashes to cunning spin shots, each player brings their unique style to the table, making every match a captivating display of skill and strategy.


The ITS Surabaya Table Tennis Competition features various categories, including singles, doubles, and mixed doubles, ensuring there's a division for players of all skill levels and preferences. Whether you're a seasoned player or a beginner taking your first steps in the sport, this competition offers an opportunity for everyone to participate and grow as table tennis enthusiasts. Beyond the intense competition, we encourage participants and spectators alike to engage in the camaraderie and sportsmanship that defines the table tennis community. Cheer for your favorite players, share tips and tricks with fellow enthusiasts, and forge new connections within the vibrant table tennis community of ITS Surabaya. To enhance your experience, we have also arranged various side activities and attractions. Explore vendor booths offering the latest table tennis equipment and accessories, participate in mini-games and challenges, or enjoy interactive exhibitions showcasing the history and evolution of table tennis.


Join us at the ITS Surabaya Table Tennis Competition and be part of this thrilling event that celebrates the spirit of competition, friendship, and the love for table tennis. We look forward to welcoming players, spectators, and table tennis enthusiasts for an unforgettable experience filled with passion and excitement. Secure your spot now and prepare to witness the future stars of table tennis!
